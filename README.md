## What is the Arco Lexicon?

The Arco Lexicon provides the **precise language of autonomous business design**.

It defines the core concepts, architectural practices, failure modes, operational metrics, and distinctions (e.g., autonomous vs. automated) that Arco Venture Studio uses to identify proven markets, reconstruct them as agentic companies, and operate them with minimal human intervention.

This repository contains the **canonical Markdown source** for every term in the Arco Lexicon. It serves as the single source of truth for:

- The concise [Arco Lexicon](https://arcoventure.studio/lexicon)
- The expanded living documentation at the [Autonomous Business Wiki / Docs](https://wiki.arcoventure.studio) (powered by Docusaurus)
- Technical notes, implementation examples, and community contributions

## Why this matters

Traditional business language is built around human coordination, meetings, approvals, and headcount.  
Autonomous business requires a new, precise vocabulary focused on agentic logic, architectural certainty, operational arbitrage, and stewardship.

The Arco Lexicon replaces vague terms with measurable, engineerable concepts.

### Selected Core Terms

- **[Agentic Core](terms/agentic-core.md)** — The reusable technical foundation shared across Arco portfolio companies.
- **[Arco Flywheel](terms/arco-flywheel.md)** — The compounding mechanism that makes each new autonomous business faster and more mature.
- **[Autonomous Business](terms/autonomous-business.md)** — A company whose core operations run independently of human labour, engineered from first principles.
- **[Context Leakage](terms/context-leakage.md)** — When an agent loses the original task intent across multiple steps.
- **[Human to Logic Ratio](terms/human-to-logic-ratio.md)** — Arco’s primary market selection metric.
- **[MTTI (Mean Time to Intervention)](terms/mtti.md)** — Target: >72 hours between human interventions.
- **[Revenue to Headcount Advantage](terms/revenue-to-headcount-advantage.md)** — Target: 10× the industry average.
